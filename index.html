How Property Tokenization Works?

Tokenization is the process of disintegrating a property into digital securities called Security Token Offerings (STO) which can then be distributed, bought or sold, in potions (like shares). Tokenization follows the outlined steps below:

  • Property due diligence check and Title Deed validation and digitization on to a public blockchain.
  • Onboarding the property into a blockchain.
  • Creating tokens with a 1:1 ratio to the property price valuation at the time of digitization.
  • Property management to determine the health of the property cash flow which will translate to flactuation of the token price.

For example, if a property worth Ksh. 8.5 million is tokenized, then there would be 8.5M STO available in the market and 1 token will be worth Ksh. 1. This property will have an tenant and will be generating a monthly rental income with a Net Operating Income (NOI) of Ksh. 30,000.

If I bought tokens worth Ksh. 100,000 (i.e 100K STOs) then I will own 1.18% of the property. This would mean I will be earning a Net Passive Income of Ksh. 354 every month from the rental income for as long as you own the STO (i.e. your lifetime).

STO Trading

STOs can be bought and sold like any other online purchase that you have done before on an eCommerce platform. One can get in and out of a real estate investment position simply by buying or selling an STO.

From the example above, we have 1 STO worth Ksh. 1. The thing with real estate is that it increases in value with time and as time goes by so does the home equity (the property's current market value). After 4 years of years of owning this STO you can decide to sell it on the market at Ksh. 1.50 per token, so with my 100K tokens I will get Ksh. 150,000 for them making a profit of Ksh. 50,000 which is a 50% return on investment (ROI).

The true value and difference new type of investment is that you will make 50% ROI i.e. Ksh. 50,000 and on top of that you will have collected a monthly rent of Ksh. 16,992 for the past 4 years. This will make your total profit to be 66,992 and your actual ROI 66.99% in 4 years.
